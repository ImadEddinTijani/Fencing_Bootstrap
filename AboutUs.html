Who are we ?

We are a team of motivated and passionate fencing trainers. Every trainer in our club has graduated with a valid fencing certification from the Canadian Board Of Fencers. We offer expert to expert and expert to novice trainings for fencer of all levels. We welcome you all to our Club and we believe that everyone has the potential to become a great fencer. Always rember that we are here for you. You can think of us as your future musketeers comrades!

Un pour tous et tous pour un !
