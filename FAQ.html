How can we help ?

There are three weapons in modern fencing: foil, épée, and sabre. Each weapon has its own rules and strategies.

The foil is a light thrusting weapon that targets the torso, but not the arms or legs.

The épée is a thrusting weapon like the foil, but heavier. In épée, the entire body is a valid target.

The sabre is a light cutting and thrusting weapon that targets the entire body above the waist, except the weapon hand.
